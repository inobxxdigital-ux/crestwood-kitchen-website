Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical kitchen remodel take?

Most full kitchen remodels take 6-10 weeks, depending on the scope of work. Simple cabinet refacing can be completed in 2-3 weeks.

What is the average cost of a kitchen remodel?

Costs vary based on size and materials. A basic remodel starts around $20,000, while a premium full remodel can range from $50,000 to $150,000+.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free in-home consultations and 3D design previews to help you visualize your new kitchen before committing.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater Midwest region including Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, Michigan, and Ohio. Contact us to confirm service in your area.

Do you handle permits?

We handle all necessary permits and inspections for your project, ensuring everything meets local building codes.

What warranty do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all workmanship, plus manufacturer warranties on materials and appliances.
